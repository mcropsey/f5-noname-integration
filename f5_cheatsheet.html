Step 5 · iRule
HSL Logger iRule iRule
noname-hsl-https-logger  ·  paste contents of send-to-noname.tcl
Fires on every HTTP request/response on juiceshop_vs. Captures full transaction, base64-encodes it, and asynchronously POSTs to the HSL pool. App traffic is never held up.
nn_poolnoname-security-hsl-https
nn_engine_hostnamemichaels-lab.nonamesec.com
nn_base64_source_key04f72d92-81f0-4c5f-8142-7f2b60142ac9
nn_payload_max_size131072 (128KB)
Step 6 · Attach
Attach iRule to juiceshop_vs The On Switch
juiceshop_vs → Resources tab → iRules → Manage → move noname-hsl-https-logger to Enabled
Nothing sends traffic to Noname until this step. Attaching the iRule here activates the entire chain.
⚠ DO NOT attach the iRule to noname-security-engine-https-vs — it will create an infinite traffic loop.

Verify

  • ▸Pools → noname-security-hsl-https → green
  • ▸Pools → noname-security-engine-https-pool → green
  • ▸iRules → noname-hsl-https-logger → Statistics → executions climbing
  • ▸Akamai UI → traffic from juiceshop appears within ~2 min

Save Config & Gotchas

  • ▸SSH → tmsh save sys config — no GUI save button on v21
  • ▸Port 443 must be open outbound: F5 → 98.88.158.72
  • ▸Payloads >128KB are skipped by the iRule
  • ▸SSL Passthrough on juiceshop_vs = no visibility (must terminate SSL)
